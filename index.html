Project Title

Digital Learning Platforms For Rural School Students In Nabha

Proposed Solution

  • Offline-first digital learning platform, simple dashboards for students and teachers.
  • Local servers connect all content and syllabus of local schools.
  • Works with low or no internet connectivity.

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: React.js / Flutter (responsive web & mobile apps, multilingual, offline support)
  • Backend: Node.js / Django (server-side logic, authentication, secure APIs)
  • Database & Cloud: PostgreSQL / MySQL, MongoDB, AWS/GCP/Azure (student data, multimedia, scalable deployment)

Feasibility & Viability

  • Technically feasible with low-cost hardware (Raspberry Pi, tablets) and open-source tools.
  • Financially viable: minimal setup cost, scalable across schools.
  • Main risks: poor internet, teacher resistance, device damage.
  • Mitigation: offline-first design, teacher training, low-cost replacements.

Impact & Benefits

  • Bridges the digital gap: rural students gain equal access to modern learning tools.
  • Empowers teachers: saves time with ready digital content, improves teaching effectiveness.
  • Sustainable & scalable: works offline, low-cost, can expand across schools and districts.

  • E-Learning in Schools During COVID-19 Pandemic in Rural Areas
